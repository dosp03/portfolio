Developing the Brand Concept

The Idea

At the start of our project we were in the position of a founding team with the task was to develop the idea for a brand from scratch, so we used brainstorming to find a promising business niche and a business concept that does not exist yet.

The idea of “Greenergym” emerged from recognizing the separate nature of two trends: Fitness and environmental protection - despite their potential for synergy. People increasingly desire an active lifestyle while also showing a growing inclination towards environmental consciousness. From our own experience and further research, we realized that traditional fitness studios often lack sustainability practices, generating evitable waste and using non-biodegradable materials.

Our brand however, is meant to be a self-sufficient system that provides a holistic and eco-friendly fitness experience, minimizes dependence on external resources and only uses sustainable and fair-trade ingredients, materials, and energy sources. This is achieved by the brand consisting of three interconnected sub-brands: GreenerGym, GreenerFoods, and GreenerWear.

Based on this initial idea, the business concept was developed in detail.

  • GreenerGym

    focuses on providing an eco-friendly fitness experience. It is the heart of the Fitnessmall where all the sport-activities take place. GreenerGym ensures that all training equipment is made from sustainable and recyclable materials, free from harmful substances and plastics. Using high-quality, durable materials like stainless steel, iron, and solid wood instead of plastic ,and generating electricity by harnessing the power generated by their cardio equipment, which is then used to operate the fitness mall, are part of their environmentally friendly self-sustainability concept. In addition Greenergym offers a mobile app that allows users to track their workouts and earn rewards for generating electricity.

  • GreenerFoods

    is a part of Greenergym that emphasizes sustainable and healthy food options, striving to combine clean and healthy ingredients with amazing taste and high protein content. To include options for every preference, they offer vegan, vegetarian, and gluten-free alternatives. Greenergym grows their own vegetables and fruits in an enclosed area of their outdoor space, which are freshly harvested and used in the delicious and nutritious GreenerFoods dishes. While not all ingredients can be sourced from their own cultivation due to seasonal variations, Greenergym ensures that the rest of the ingredients are obtained from organic and regional sources. They also partner with sustainable snack manufacturers to reduce packaging and offer unpackaged options.

  • GreenerWear

    offers sportswear made from recyclable, recycled, and fair-trade materials. The sportswear collection of Greenergym's apparel branch "GreenerWear" includes items made from organic cotton that stems from controlled organic farming and fibers derived from PET bottles. They prioritize sustainable and ethical production practices and aim to expand their own production while also collaborating with partner companies. The items from the clothing store can be obtained locally as well as in the online shop or the app.

Unique Selling Point

On the basis of the preceding Market Analysis the following aspects could be worked out as the unique positioning of Greenergym

Sustainable Equipment

Greenergym ensures that all training equipment is made from sustainable and recyclable materials, avoiding plastics and harmful substances. They primarily use stainless steel, iron, and solid wood instead of plastic, making the equipment more durable and less prone to wear and tear.

Green & Renewable Energy

Greenergym not only eliminates energy-consuming devices but also harnesses the energy generated by customers when using cardio equipment thanks to built-in generators, which is regularly rewarded with incentives. Additionally, the roof is covered with photovoltaic panels, generating even more electricity to power the facility and offset cooling needs.

Eco-friendly Sanitation

Greenergym promotes green hygiene practices by using recycled toilet paper, paper towels, and biodegradable trash bags. Their cleaning and disinfecting products are derived from plant-based substances and meet high-quality and cleanliness standards.

Farm-to-Table Approach

Greenergym incorporates an outdoor area for growing fresh vegetables and fruits, which are then used in their healthy and delicious GreenerFoods dishes. They prioritize organic and locally sourced ingredients, providing vegan, vegetarian, and gluten-free alternatives.

Sustainable Apparel

Greenergym offers GreenerWear sports clothing made from not only recyclable but also recycled and fair-trade materials, such as organic cotton and polyester fibers derived from PET bottles, because clothes also play an important part in the fitness experience.

Continous Improvement

While recognizing their imperfections, Greenergym takes its environmental responsibility seriously. They strive to continually improve their sustainability practices, aim to become completely self-sufficient in energy, achieve carbon neutrality, and eventually grow their own GreenerFoods ingredients.

Core values

Passion for Sports

We celebrate the joy, energy, and positive impact that sports bring to individuals and communities and believe in the power of sports to uplift and unite people.

Environmental Conciousness

We deeply care about the health of our planet and recognize the connection of the environment and sports. We integrate sustainability into every aspect of our operations and inspire others to do the same.

Health and Well-being

We prioritize the well-being of individuals, understanding that personal health and the health of the planet are intertwined. We strive to create a world where everyone has access to clean air, clean water, and a safe environment for physical activity.

Innovation and Excellence

We constantly seek innovative solutions and strive for excellence in everything we do. We aim to set new benchmarks in sustainable sports practices and inspire others through our actions. Most modern approach.

Vision

To create a global movement where sports and environmental consciousness go hand in hand, where every individual can engage in physical activity while contributing to a cleaner and greener planet. We envision a world where sports facilities, equipment, and events are designed with sustainability in mind, setting a new standard for the sports industry.

Mission

To unite the environment and sports, promoting a sustainable and healthy lifestyle while inspiring individuals, especially the younger generation, to prioritize environmental protection for their own well-being and that of future generations.

Definition of purpose

Defining the exact purpose of both the website and the app was crucial before starting the website and app-design, because it gave us a potential functions.

  • Website

    • Function: The website primarily serves an informative function, providing users with an introduction to the company, its products, and services.
    • Purpose: The website aims to familiarize users with the brand, educate them about the offerings, and guide them towards a conversion or specific action, such as signing up for a membership or visiting the physical fitness mall.
    • Touchpoints: The website acts as a touchpoint at the early stages of the customer journey, capturing users' attention, generating interest, and providing the necessary information to make an informed decision.

  • App

    • Function: The app focuses on providing interactive and personalized experiences to users who have already shown interest or become customers.
    • Purpose: The app aims to enhance user engagement, facilitate access to specific services or features, and enable ongoing interaction with the brand. It is meant to serve as a central hub for users, allowing them to track their energy generation through cardio machines, redeem rewards, explore workout plans, shop for eco-friendly products, and access restaurant menus.

User Interface Design: Mobile App

Home - Dashboard

Presents the user with important shortcuts to different sections of the app. It includes a circular diagram representing the amount of electricity generated toward the next reward. Below the diagram, important events and information are displayed, such as scheduled workouts, daily statistics, and daily restaurant offerings. These elements serve as shortcuts to different sections of the app.

Onlineshop

The shop page serves as a redirective gateway page to the GreenerWear- and GreenerFoods-Store, which are separated for a more intuitive understanding and orientation. Once in the chosen Shop, users can chose between different categories, search for keywords, see their cart or wishlist and view more details on the product-screen.

Workouts & Plans

The user can access a variety of workouts that can be added to their personal training plan or started immediately. Each workout is represented by an image, title, description, and key details. The top part of the screen allows users to filter workouts based on different training types.

Cardio Tracking

The cardio tracking feature allows users to connect to a cardio machine in the gym and see live measurements of various parameters, which are later saved into the statistics section in their profile. After scanning the QR-code on the respective machine, the app starts tracking the cardio workout. If the user clicks on the Pause-button, any menu item or the arrow back, the app will direct the user to a different screen after confirmation to proceed. i In this case the tracking will be stopped. The feature is accessed by pressing the green "+" Button in the menu or selecting "Cardio" in the workout selection.
