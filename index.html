Projects.

Explore my most recent and innovative projects developed during my academic pursuits.

Kaya

Business concept UX/UI Design Branding

The aim of this project, which was the final submission for the module "Screen Design" of my studies, was to develop a concept and a Digital Brand Design for a hotel of our choice, including a prototype for a responsive website design. Our work resulted in the creation of "Kaya" - a five star luxury wellness retreat in Santorini, focusing on the holisitic relaxation of mind, body and soul.

Lieber Regional

UX/UI Design Cross-Media Marketing Campaign

The background of this project was to develop and pitch a cross-media-marketing campaign with the aim of raising appreciation for local farmers. We developed consistent storytelling and a plan for our campaign called "Lieber regional", developed a concept for a digital marketplace, where farmers have the opportunity to sell their products to locals easily as well as printmedia to promote our message on multiple channels.

Greenergym

Business concept Corporate Design UX/UI Design Branding

Greenergym is an eco-friendly fitness mall consisting of 3 areas: a gym, a clothing store, and a food establishment. The project's scope included both the development of a business concept and a corporate identity for 'Greenergym' with a full corporate Design. This involved developing printed materials, a website, and a concept for a fitness app that enhances the overall visitor experience of the mall, which was also developed as a clickable high-fidelity prototype.

About me

My name is Dorothee, and I'm 22 years old, currently studying Digital Media Marketing at Hochschule Kaiserslautern. I am currently in the application process for a mandatory internship, which will be followed by my Bachelor's thesis.

Through my studies, I have gained knowledge and experience in Web, UX, and UI design, programming, and marketing. However, the UX/UI aspect of my studies brings me the most joy, and it is clear to me which direction I want to pursue for my future.
